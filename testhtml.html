Chapter 1: Discrete Fourier Transform

The Discrete Fourier Transform (DFT) converts a sequence of values into components of different frequencies.

Mathematical Background

Mathematically, the discrete Fourier transform is represented as:

$$ X_k = \sum_{n=0}^{N-1} x_n \cdot e^{-i \cdot 2\pi \cdot k \cdot n / N} $$

Implementation


import numpy as np
x = np.array([0.0, 1.0, 0.0, 0.0])
X = np.fft.fft(x)
print(X)
